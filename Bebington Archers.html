Welcome to Bebington Archers

About Our Club

Bebington Archers is a friendly club that supports everyone from complete beginners to competition archers and of all ages.

Archers in the club shoot recurve, compound, barebow and longbow.

Learn to Shoot

Our beginner's courses are the perfect way to start your archery journey. We provide all the equipment and coaching you need.

Join Our Community

Become a member and enjoy our club sessions, tournaments, and a supportive community of fellow archers.

Beginner Information

Our beginner courses are a fantastic introduction to the sport. Over a series of sessions, you will learn the fundamentals of safe archery, including shooting technique, equipment handling, and range etiquette. All necessary equipment is provided.

What to Expect

  • Certified coaches will guide you through every step.
  • All equipment, including bows, arrows, and safety gear, is provided.
  • Learn the basics of recurve and compound bows.
  • Understand range safety rules and protocols.

Our next course starts soon! Please check the contact page to inquire about availability and booking.

Member Information

Membership is open to all archers who have completed a beginner's course or can demonstrate competence and safety. As a member, you'll have access to our club sessions, coaching, and social events.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to our indoor and outdoor shooting ranges.
  • Opportunity to participate in club tournaments and competitions.
  • Ongoing coaching and development opportunities.
  • Social events and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

To apply for membership, please contact our membership secretary via the contact page.

Coaching at Bebington Archers

Our club has a dedicated team of qualified coaches who are passionate about helping archers improve. Whether you're a beginner looking for guidance or an experienced archer aiming for a specific goal, our coaches can provide personalized support.

Meet Our Coaches

Our coaches are certified by the National Governing Body for Archery and have extensive experience in all disciplines. They are available during scheduled club sessions to offer advice and structured coaching.

One-to-One Coaching

Private coaching sessions can be arranged for members who wish to work on specific aspects of their technique. Please speak to one of our coaches at the range or contact us to discuss your requirements.

Ask the Virtual Coach ✨

Get a quick coaching tip from our AI-powered virtual assistant. Ask about technique, form, or practice drills.

Safeguarding Policy

Bebington Archers is committed to creating a safe and positive environment for all participants, especially children and vulnerable adults. We have a strict safeguarding policy in place that all members, coaches, and volunteers are expected to follow.

Our Commitment

  • All coaches and volunteers are DBS-checked and receive regular safeguarding training.
  • We have a designated Safeguarding Officer who is the first point of contact for any concerns.
  • Our policies are reviewed regularly to ensure they meet the highest standards.
  • We encourage everyone to report any concerns, no matter how small.

A full copy of our Safeguarding Policy is available upon request. Please contact our Safeguarding Officer via the details on the contact page.
